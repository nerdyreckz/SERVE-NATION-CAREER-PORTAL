"Serve with integrity, lead with wisdom — persistence turns aspiration into service."

1) Basic Information About the Exam

  • Conducted by: Union Public Service Commission (UPSC)
  • Frequency: Annual
  • Exam mode: Prelims + Mains + Interview
  • Age limits: Gen 21–32; OBC +3; SC/ST +5
  • Education: Graduate or final-year
  • Attempts: Gen 6; OBC 9; SC/ST unlimited
  • Months: Notification (Feb), Prelims (Jun), Mains (Sep–Oct)
  • Centres: 70+ across India

2) Result Criteria

  • Prelims: Screening (CSAT qualifying)
  • Mains: Written papers (1750 marks)
  • Interview: 275 marks
  • Final merit: Mains + Interview

3) Scope (Posts, Promotions, Salary & Nature of Work)

  • Services: IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, IAAS & others
  • Cadre: All-India Services get state cadres
  • Promotion chain: SDO → DC → Commissioner → Secretary
  • Pay levels: 10–18 (varies by seniority)
