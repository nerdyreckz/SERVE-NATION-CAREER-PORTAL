Indian Foreign Service (IFS)

"The Foreign Service is not merely a career; it is a calling to serve your nation with dignity, shaping international relations and upholding India's values across the world."


1. Basic Information About the Exam

  • Conducting Authority: UPSC through Civil Services Examination (CSE)
  • Cadre Type: All India Service (Central Service) under MEA
  • Exam Frequency: Once per year
  • Exam Mode: Offline
  • Age Limit:
    • General: 21–32
    • OBC: 21–35
    • SC/ST: 21–37
  • Educational Qualification: Bachelor's degree
  • Attempt Limits:
    • General: 6
    • OBC: 9
    • SC/ST: Unlimited until 37
  • Notification Release: Jan–Feb
  • Stages: Prelims → Mains → Interview
  • Centres: 25+ across India

2. Result Criteria & Merit Selection

Selection Process Breakdown:

  • Prelims: Qualifying only
  • Mains: Counts toward merit
  • Interview: Personality assessment
  • Final Merit: 90% Mains + 10% Interview
  • IFS Allocation based on:
    • Rank
    • Vacancies
    • Preference
